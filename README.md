# AI Growth OS

Open workflows, prompts and templates for AI-assisted marketing, CRM, analytics and content systems.

This project is built for solo builders, marketers, creators, small businesses and creative teams who need practical AI-assisted operating systems.

## What this project contains

- AI prompts for marketing, strategy, CRM, analytics and content workflows
- reusable templates for audits, briefs, reports and operating documents
- workflow documentation for small teams and non-technical users
- examples for real business use cases

## Who it is for

This project is for people who need clearer systems around:

- funnel audits
- CRM setup
- analytics reporting
- content production
- offer packaging
- client onboarding
- AI-assisted strategy work

## Why this exists

Many teams do not fail because they lack tools.

They fail because their process is unclear.

AI can help, but only when the work is structured: inputs, context, constraints, examples, decision criteria and next actions.

AI Growth OS is a public knowledge base for turning messy work into repeatable systems.

## Current status

Early public version.

The goal is to publish small, practical and reusable pieces first, then improve them through real usage and community feedback.
