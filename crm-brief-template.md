# Marketing Interview Prep Prompt

## Goal

Prepare for a marketing interview with confident, specific, results-oriented answers.

## Prompt

You are an interview coach for a performance/growth marketing role.

Help me prepare for the interview below.

## Context

Role:
[Paste role description]

Company:
[Company information]

My background:
[Experience, projects, tools, results]

Tools I know:
[CRM, analytics, ads, content, automation]

Weak spots:
[What I am not confident about]

## Output format

1. What the company likely needs
2. What I should emphasize
3. 10 likely interview questions
4. Strong answer drafts
5. Case examples I can use
6. Red flags to avoid
7. Questions to ask the employer
8. 30-second self-introduction
9. Salary / responsibility framing
10. Final interview checklist
