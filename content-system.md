# Funnel Audit Prompt

## Goal

Audit a marketing funnel and find the biggest conversion problems, missing assets, weak messages, and next actions.

## Prompt

You are a senior growth strategist.

Analyze the funnel below and identify the biggest problems affecting conversion, lead quality, and sales efficiency.

## Context

Business:
[Describe the business]

Offer:
[Describe the product/service]

Target audience:
[Describe the audience]

Current funnel:
[Traffic source → landing page → lead magnet/form → CRM/sales → purchase]

Current metrics:
- Traffic:
- CTR:
- Conversion to lead:
- Cost per lead:
- Lead qualification rate:
- Sales conversion:
- Average deal size:
- LTV:
- ROMI:

Known problems:
[List problems]

Constraints:
[Budget, team, tech stack, deadlines]

## Output format

1. TL;DR
2. Funnel map
3. Biggest bottlenecks
4. Message problems
5. Tracking problems
6. CRM/sales handoff problems
7. Quick wins
8. 14-day action plan
9. Metrics to track
10. Risks

## Rules

- Be specific.
- Do not give generic advice.
- Separate assumptions from facts.
- Prioritize actions by impact and effort.
